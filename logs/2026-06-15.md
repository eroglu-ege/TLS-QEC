## UNDERSTOOD
- H is block-diagonal in excitation number: N=0,1,2 subspaces
- All dynamics live in the N=1 subspace {|eg>, |ge>}
- Resonant case: eigenvalues ±0.1 = ±g (vacuum Rabi splitting)
- Dispersive case: eigenvalues ±0.1118 = ±Omega_R = ±sqrt(Delta^2/4 + g^2)
- Solomon equations = Lindblad with coherences set to zero
- Solomon breaks down when g/gamma_t >> 1 (coherences survive)
