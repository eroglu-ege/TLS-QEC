## UNDERSTOOD

### JC model physics
- Photons do NOT diverge under continuous drive — saturate at n_ss = g^2*Omega^2/...
- g^2 in numerator: no coupling = no photons (drive is on qubit, not resonator directly)
- Omega^2 scaling: doubling drive quadruples photon number
- kappa plays exact role of gamma_t from TLS model
- g/kappa plays exact role of g/gamma_t — same Solomon threshold

### Purcell effect
- Enhanced qubit decay when resonant with cavity: gamma_P = 4g^2/kappa (at Delta=0)
- Lorentzian in detuning: drops by 100x for Delta = 5*kappa
- Bad cavity (kappa>>g): Purcell regime, Solomon exact, exponential ADC pulse
- Strong coupling (g>>kappa): Rabi oscillations, Solomon fails, multiple ADC bursts
- Threshold g/kappa ~ 0.5-1: matches TLS model threshold g/gamma_t ~ 0.216-0.5

### Initialization vs active reset
- Initialization: set state once at t=0, evolve freely
- Active reset: every dt, forcibly return TLS to P_t=1, leave qubit, evolve again
- Active reset models TLS continuously re-excited by environment
- Qubit accumulates population as staircase over N cycles
- TLS shows sawtooth (reset to 1, drain, reset...)
- At large g/gamma_t: qubit fills faster (Lindblad) vs Solomon due to coherent bursts

### Transition frequency vs Rabi frequency
- omega_q: energy gap, set by fabrication, ~GHz, property of qubit alone
- Omega: drive Rabi rate, set by microwave power, tunable
- g: vacuum Rabi rate, set by coupling geometry, fixed per device
- Analogy: omega_q=pitch of tuning fork, Omega=how hard you strike it

### Realistic parameters (5 GHz transmon, ~50 mK)
- n_th = n_thermal(5.0, 0.050) ~ 0.05
- T2 = T1/2 → gamma_phi = 2/T2 - gamma = 0.03
- g/kappa ~ 1-100 in circuit QED (already in strong coupling)
- Dangerous TLS: g/gamma_t ~ 0.5-5 (Solomon breakdown zone)

### Data findings
- Driven JC: analytic formula confirmed to ratio=1.0000 at all Omega
- Low coupling nth=0.05: at g/kappa=0.1, peak_n = 0.050 = n_th (thermal floor)
- Purcell kicks in at g/kappa~0.36 where gamma_P = gamma (doubling qubit decay)
- Total photons 0.35-0.61: 35-61% of qubit energy reaches ADC
- Detuning sweep: Lorentzian confirmed, Purcell drops 100x for Delta=5*kappa
- Coherence grows monotonically with g/kappa (same as TLS model)
